Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedinly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy way to add functionality or change the looks of a blog

Robust Content Management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speen in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly, no matter where your readers are, keeping your site competitive.

Free, Open, Simple

Blogr is a free open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as codes syntax highlighing, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn

Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing the most complicated sites
